Accomplishments

Eagle Scout

November 2020

The rank of Eagle Scout is the highest honor achievable in Scouts BSA. One of the requirements for Eagle is the Eagle Scout Service Project, in which a scout "demonstrates leadership of others while performing a project for the benefit of their community." My Eagle Project benefited CommpassionNWA and Feed The 479, a thrift store and food pantry, with 3 desks for their intake area. The desks consist of plywood, 2x4s, and laminated tops. During this project, I learned a lot about woodworking, but I learned more about leading a team, delegation, and project management.

Volunteer Work

Cross Church Media Team

2015 to present

Starting in eighth grade, I have been volunteering on the Media Team at Cross Church. I started on the Student Media team, where I would run lights using a Vista S3 board and occasionally graphics using ProPresenter 6. When I graduated High School, I started volunteering on the main Media Team, running lights on Sunday mornings with the grandMA full-size. A few years ago we upgraded to the grandMA3 compact XT board. I still volunteer weekly on Sundays and Tuesdays running lights for those services.
